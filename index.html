    • Durable and Waterproof Watches for Daily Use

    When choosing a durable and waterproof watch for daily use, you can consider things like the watch's water resistance rating, material, and design.

    Water resistance rating
  • Watches with a water resistance of 30 ATM more are considered completely waterproof.
  • Watches with different water resistance levels are suitable for different activities, from everyday wear to water sports.
    • Examples
  • The Rolex Deep Sea Special is a diving watch that has been tested at depths of up to 10,916 meters.
  • The ISO-2281 standard defines "water-resistant" watches, and the ISO-6425 standard defines "diving" watches.
